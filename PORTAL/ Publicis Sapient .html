Company : Publicis Sapient
Company Name : Publicis Sapient
Nature Of Business : IT / Consulting
Designation : Associate Software Developer
Description :

Descriptor:

As a ASDII – Product Engineering, you would be part of elite group of young engineers interested in working in the space of Digital Business Transformation part of a world class product engineering team. You will be responsible for delivering programs in a dynamic, fast paced, scalable agile environment. We are looking for candidates who have a strong background in application development.

 

Job Summary:

Associate Software Development Engineers(ASDII) are the bright talented and motivated young minds with strong technical skills, developing software applications and services, that make life easier for customers. The ASDII is expected to work with an agile team to develop, test, and maintain digital business applications.

  • Assisting in the collection and documentation of user's requirements, development of user stories, and estimates.
  • Design, develop, and unit test applications in accordance with established standards.
  • Participating in peer-reviews of solution designs and related code.
  • Package and support deployment of releases.
  • Analyzing and resolving technical and application problems.
  • Adhering to high-quality development principles while delivering solutions on-time and on-budget.
  • Preparing reports, manuals and other documentation on the status, operation and maintenance of software.

 

Skills Required:

Technical

  • Solid understanding of object oriented programming concepts
  • Data Structures, Algorithms, Java, C/C++, PHP
  • Good understanding of relational and/or NoSQL databases
  • Understanding of presentation layer (HTML, CSS, JS)

Behavioral

  • Quick learner
  • Experimentation and innovative mindset
  • Ability to adapt to change
  • Collaborative
  • Strive to establish and maintain a culture of open and honest communication.
  • Provide and seek constructive feedback on a regular basis.
  • Focus on continuous improvement and growth
  • Can work independently and in teams

 

Career Roadmap:

You would be part of a 24-month rotation program wherein you would be exposed to different phases of software development lifecycle – the tools, processes, methodology and technology used in those phases. First phase of rotation would be assigned, second rotation would be based on various choices available:

  1. Movement to different industry vertical
  2. Movement to different technology stack or both

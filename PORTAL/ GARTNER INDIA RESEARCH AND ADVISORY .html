Company : GARTNER INDIA RESEARCH AND ADVISORY
Company Name : GARTNER INDIA RESEARCH AND ADVISORY
Nature Of Business : Research and Advisory
Designation : Business Associate
Description :

The Gartner LEADIT Rotational Development (LEADIT RD) program provides a unique opportunity for a small number of highly talented individuals to gain exposure to our analytical, data science and software engineering teams over the course of two to four years. LEADIT RDs rotate through assignments as part of these teams, gaining exposure to some of Gartner’s most important business opportunities through analytics and technology. Through challenging rotations, LEADIT RDs will develop well-rounded skills and an understanding of the interdependencies between different service areas, while gaining exposure to advanced analytics, data science initiatives, data engineering and software engineering functions. LEADIT RDs also have access to several senior business leaders from diverse and varied backgrounds, who will be invested in the growth and development of the LEADIT RDs.  As a result, LEADIT RDs have the opportunity to develop a network of contacts and relationships throughout the organization.  
 
What you’ll do:  

 Apply Natural Language Processing to improve our ability to synthesize unstructured information and predictive modelling to develop intelligence to recommend the “Next Best Action” for the client  

 Surface actionable insights from large volumes of data by leveraging statistical concepts; automating wherever possible to allow for scale

 Use Machine Learning to develop self-correcting algorithms to improve our recommendations for Services teams to increase client retention

 Work with the product owner to define product roadmap, build systems and tools, and actively review development priorities in collaboration with engineering, and business teams to deliver measurable impact.

 Build pipelines and scalable data models to allow for consolidation of large data sets and perform analysis

 Design, implement unit, integration testing and build web-based applications and services

 Communicate to business & service leaders recommended actions based on findings  What you’ll need:  

 Relevant degree in Computer Science Engineering /Electrical Engineering/ Electronics Engineering / Applied Mathematics and Business Analytics

 Practical, intuitive problem solver with an ability to translate quantitative analysis into actionable business strategies

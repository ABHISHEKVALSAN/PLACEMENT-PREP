Software Development Engineer I – Rivigo India About us
Founded in 2014 and listed among the world’s top emerging businesses, RIVIGO is writing the future of logistics with a global-first driver relay model enabled by a unique mix of next generation technology and operational excellence.
Through the relay model, we have fundamentally changed the truck driver’s job from a ‘months-away-from-home’ job to a ‘day’ job. Unlike conventional truck drivers, relay pilots drive from pitstop to pitstop (under 300 kms) and return to their families the same day, thus initiating a huge cycle of social change.
Our customers leverage 50-70% reduction in transit time and 100% precision and reliability to reinvent their business models – by serving new markets, reducing inventory and significantly cutting costs. With ground-breaking technology solutions and advanced data analytics, we are a unique logistics network that aspires to be India’s largest new age logistics platform. We are contributing to building the new India by defining the next-generation trucking industry with accurate predictions, elevated levels of efficiency, simple automated operations, and by making logistics human. What You’ll Do
As a Software Developer, you will build scalable systems which are capable of crunching and storing large amounts of data (from IOT, sensors) in real time; build intuitive and intelligent applications that can automate decision making.
• Develop responsive web applications and scalable services
• Build scalable data platforms
• Ensure functional, performance and security requirements are met
• Maintain quality and high standards in coding
• Collaborate with the product and engineering teams to translate feature requirements from design to a deployable unit
You are expected to be:
• Customer Oriented: You are obsessed with providing the highest quality product and experience to the customer.
• A Go-Getter: You are highly driven individual who goes that extra mile to deliver an outstanding product to the business team.
• Analytical and Problem Solver: You understand the needs and requirements and conceptualize and design solutions for the problems.
• Adaptable: You demonstrate the ability to work in a fast paced and hyper-growth environment where the requirements are constantly changing.
Technology we work on:
• Java Big Data EcoSystem (Hadoop, Spark)
• Time Series Analytics (Druid, InfluxDB)
• Machine Resource Management (Mesos)
• Machine Learning, Demand Forecasting, Route Optimizations
• Python, Sql, Mongo (Pandas, Flask, Bash, Anaconda)

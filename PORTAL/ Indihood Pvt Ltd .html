We are looking for a few great engineers:
● With strong CS fundamentals in algorithms, data structures, and systems programming
● Love coding and converting ideas to large systems
● Instinctively design for scale and create from scratch
● Passionate in solving complex real-world problems
Any of the following are a big plus. Experience or deep passion for:
● Large scale distributed systems like No-SQL DBs, graph DBs, hadoop/ mapreduce
● Machine learning for classification, pattern matching, non-linear optimization
● User facing systems for quasi-literate and under educated
Our approach is to reuse best of breed open systems, while filling those technology gaps in the internet
ecosystem (including Google, Amazon, MS, FB, India Stack) that when filled will empower everyone in the
world to meaningfully use the internet for problems that are critical to people’s lives.
Indihood does not have coders. We have software engineers who are responsible for ideas, responsible for
turning ideas into design, design into code, code into user facing features that will impact users at scale.
The engineers building this platform are pioneers from Google who invented, built and launched systems that
touched a billion lives - including Google’s Map Maker, Google Finance, Google Transliteration, YouTube
community translation, Crowdsourced machine translation training. You will be working shoulder to shoulder
with these engineers.
